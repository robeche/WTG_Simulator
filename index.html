Actuator disc theory

The rotor is modeled as a permeable actuator disc that slows the air down. The axial induction a measures how much the wind speed is reduced at the rotor plane relative to the free stream. The stream tube contracts upstream and expands in the wake because the air slows down (mass conservation). The Betz limit is the value of a that maximizes the extracted power.
